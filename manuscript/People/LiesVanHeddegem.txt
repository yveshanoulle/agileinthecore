# Who is Lies Van Heddegem? {#LiesVanHeddegem}

![Lies](images/LiesVanHeddegem.jpg)

I asked Lies as a start for the book, as she is leading agile in the core in 2015.


**What is something people usually don’t know about you but has influenced you in who you are?**


I studied ‘sport science’ and worked 10 years in sports industry (mainly
tennis federation). 

**If would not be doing the job you are doing now, what would have become of you?**

I would for sure be involved in another exciting project that was crossing my path.

**What is your biggest challenge and why is it a good thing for you?** 

Together with all coaches I would like to make 'Agile in the core’ the biggest, best, and most innovating Agile training and coaching business in Europe. It is good for me because I like to put my energy in a challenging project!

**What drives you?**

My enduring hunger to experience new things.

**What is your biggest achievement?**

That I do always the things that I like. So, if I would die tomorrow, I have no regrets.

**Is there a piece of music that has a special meaning for you?**

There are a lot of songs that reminds me on special occasions, persons, experiences, ….

**What is the last book you have read?**

A book of Santa Montefiore

**What question do you think I should also ask and what is the answer?**

What brings happiness in your life besides your work? Partner, sports, travelling,
family/friends

**Whom do you think we should ask next from agile in the core?**

[Nick Oostvogels](#NickOostvogels), because he was the only coach who was not present in Rotterdam..
